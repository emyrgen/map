CMP Alternative Thesis

Replace harsh slurry chemistry with controlled magnetic finishing.

EMYRGEN™ MAP is presented as a chemical-free, ultra-precision finishing pathway designed to reduce or replace selected CMP-style polishing steps where customer testing confirms performance, yield, and integration value.

MAP uses a magnetic field, an inductor tool, nanoscale magnetic iron-diamond abrasive media, and controlled scanning to condition the surface without a conventional rigid tool or chemical-heavy slurry process.

Core technology message

Magnetic field + nanoscale abrasive media + automatic surface scanning.

EMYRGEN™ MAP uses nanoscale magnetic iron-diamond abrasives guided by a magnetic inductor and automatic surface scanning to deliver ultra-precision finishing for semiconductors, optical glass, and other defect-sensitive surfaces.
