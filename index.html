Technology

Magnetic abrasive polishing turns a field into a precision finishing tool.

The supplied POLIMAG documentation describes a magnetic field that structures ferroabrasive powder into an elastic polishing brush. The brush can conform to surfaces, polish difficult geometries, and modify surface layers with low thermal impact.

Magnetic-abrasive polishing uses magnetic force, ferroabrasive particles, process fluid, and controlled motion to condition surfaces without relying on a rigid cutting tool.

Core mechanism

An elastic abrasive brush for defect-sensitive surfaces.

Instead of forcing a fixed abrasive tool against the workpiece, MAP forms a controlled magnetic-abrasive working zone. That working zone can remove or condition material at micro/nano scale while reducing thermal and mechanical damage risk compared with aggressive grinding or chemical finishing routes.
