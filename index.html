AI Infrastructure Thesis

AI is no longer only a software race. It is a surface-control race.

AI accelerators, custom silicon, advanced packaging, optics, photonics, power electronics, and cooling systems are all becoming more sensitive to microscopic defects. When compute density rises, the physical surface becomes a performance and reliability constraint.

V1.4 moves the semiconductor and optics story to the front: MAP should be framed as a surface-integrity platform for the physical AI supply chain, not just as an industrial polishing method.
More AI compute means more wafers, more advanced packaging, more optics, more heat, and more reliability stress. Surface integrity becomes a hidden bottleneck.

CMP Alternative Thesis

Replace harsh slurry chemistry with controlled magnetic finishing.

The V1.4 website should describe EMYRGEN™ MAP as a chemical-free, ultra-precision finishing pathway designed to reduce or replace selected CMP-style polishing steps where customer testing confirms performance, yield, and integration value.

MAP uses a magnetic field, an inductor tool, nanoscale magnetic iron-diamond abrasive media, and controlled scanning to condition the surface without a conventional rigid tool or chemical-heavy slurry process.

Core technology message

Magnetic field + nanoscale abrasive media + automatic surface scanning.

V1.4 should make the mechanism clear in one sentence: EMYRGEN™ MAP uses nanoscale magnetic iron-diamond abrasives guided by a magnetic inductor and automatic surface scanning to deliver ultra-precision finishing for semiconductors, optical glass, and other defect-sensitive surfaces.

Why this matters

Less chemical burdenPosition MAP as a cleaner pathway that may reduce chemical slurry dependence, disposal burden, residue, and environmental load.
Lower defect and residue riskConnect impurity reduction and surface smoothing directly to wafer yield, long-term chip reliability, and AI accelerator lifespan.
Precision without overclaimingUse “designed to replace or reduce selected CMP steps” rather than claiming full fab-scale replacement before customer validation.
Partner-ready framingInvite pilot testing, licensing, integration, and co-development with chip, optics, equipment, and materials partners.
