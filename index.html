CMP Alternative Thesis

Replace harsh slurry chemistry with controlled magnetic finishing.

Chemical mechanical polishing is essential in advanced manufacturing, but it can involve slurry chemistry, residue, waste, cost, and process complexity. EMYRGEN™ MAP targets selected finishing applications where magnetic abrasive polishing can deliver a cleaner path after validation.

MAP uses a magnetic field, an inductor tool, nanoscale magnetic iron-diamond abrasive media, and controlled scanning to condition surfaces without a conventional rigid polishing tool or chemical-heavy slurry process.

Technology platform

Magnetic field + nanoscale abrasive media + automatic surface scanning.

EMYRGEN™ MAP uses nanoscale magnetic iron-diamond abrasives guided by a magnetic inductor and automatic surface scanning to deliver ultra-precision finishing for semiconductors, optical glass, and other defect-sensitive surfaces.
